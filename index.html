Kikoru Shinomiya

Kikoru is a lead character from the show "Kaiju No. 8". She is outgoing and excelled in the defense force. Her father is the leader of the defense force so she always is trying to prove herself. This comes without fail as she becomes the strongest rookie of the first season and dominates the show with strength and great personality.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa is one of the main characters from "Attack On Titan", which is a show based purely on survival. Mikasa is someone who is physically and mentally strong, she easily became one of the strongest people in the show. She is very cool and level headed, never letting her suuroundings get the better of her.

Rukia Kuchiki

Rukia is a strong lead from the show "Bleach". Now "Bleach" is my favorite show, and Rukia is one of my favorite characters. She is a soul reaper and her zanpaktou possesses ice elements. She is strong and level-headed.

Tohru Honda

Tohru Honda is the main character in the show "Fruits Basket". This character makes you question yourself. She is so kind and considerate of every person around her, she makes you look at your own personality and question if you could ever be THAT kind.

Momo Ayase

Momo is the main character in the show "Dandadan". She has special telekinetic abilities that make her a special force within the show. She is funny, witty, and charasmatic. She shows how to be strong and serious but also funny and down to earth.
